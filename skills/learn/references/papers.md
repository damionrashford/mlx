# Papers

Key papers to read (or have an agent summarize) as you progress.

## Must-Read Papers

| Paper | Year | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Attention Is All You Need | 2017 | The transformer architecture — foundation of modern AI |
| BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers | 2018 | Bidirectional pretraining for NLP |
| Language Models are Few-Shot Learners (GPT-3) | 2020 | In-context learning, scaling |
| LoRA: Low-Rank Adaptation of Large Language Models | 2021 | Efficient fine-tuning |
| Training Language Models to Follow Instructions (InstructGPT) | 2022 | RLHF for alignment |
| Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) | 2023 | Simpler alignment without reward model |
| Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models | 2020 | Compute-optimal training |
